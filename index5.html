Emotional Bullying

What is Emotional Bullying?

Emotional bullying is when a person tries to get what they want by making others feel angry or afraid. Emotional bullying isn't just seen on the playground; emotional bullying, although likely subtler, is seen in adult relationships and workplaces too.
